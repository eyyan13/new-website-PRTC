Patterns for Truck & Bus

R187 / R187 (SUPER)

V-Steel Rib Super R187 is a commercial-grade tire designed for light and medium trucks, offering enhanced durability, traction, and comfort for highway applications.
ECOPIA

ECOPIA tyres return more energy back to the tyre instead of generating heat and compromising fuel efficiency. Ecopia tyres help channel water away from the tyre through circumferential grooves.
G610

G610 is a commercial truck tire designed for durability and performance in various applications. It is suitable for all positions on trucks, making it a versatile choice for various commercial needs.

R294 is a highway terrain, all-season tire designed for commercial vehicles, specifically for the steer axle. Its five-rib pattern ensures even load distribution across any axle position, enhancing stability and handling.
